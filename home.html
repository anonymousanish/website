    OUR HISTORY

    When Youwe was not yet established and all the members were operating under a different NGO, the members did more than 35 on-ground campaigns in 4 different cities in Bihar.
    That includes Sex Education sessions, Periods of Awareness, Women's Safety Awareness Campaigns, and food/cloth distribution campaigns.
    We have also been socially presenting our works including 5 webinars online on issues like Cybersecurity, Mental Health, Domestic Violence and LGBTQ.

     

    OUR MISSION

    Youwe is an NGO working with the central goal of Gender neutral laws and the growth of reasonable sex education in India for every age group the main motive is to encourage the youth to be more active towards the wrong around them and encourage awareness and seeking help for victims of several distinct crimes.

    Our Motto: Bringing the change - You & We together.
     

    OUR TEAM

    Ever since the formation of Youwe with Mohit Ashok Gupta as the President and Shubham as the board of director and the rest of the members with our volunteers have achieved 35 ground campaigns, which include cloth distribution, food distribution, education campaigns, covering government schools, slums, and some centers of other NGOs.
