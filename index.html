OUR HISTORY

When Youwe was not yet established and all the members were operating under a different NGO, the members did more than 35 on-ground campaigns in 4 different cities in Bihar. That includes Sex Education sessions, Periods of Awareness, Women's Safety Awareness Campaigns, and food/cloth distribution campaigns. We have also been socially presenting our works including 5 webinars online on issues like Cybersecurity, Mental Health, Domestic Violence and LGBTQ.

Youwe is an NGO working with the central goal of Gender neutral laws and the growth of reasonable sex education in India for every age group the main motive is to encourage the youth to be more active towards the wrong around them and encourage awareness and seeking help for victims of several distinct crimes.

Our Motto: Bringing the change - You & We together.

Ever since the formation of YouWe with Mohit Ashok Gupta as the President and Shubham, Survi Keshri & Vikash Kumar as the Board of Directors who previously have worked under different NGOs effectively and are continuing to lead us all with the same spirit.
